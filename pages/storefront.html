Book House & Coffee Place

Books. Coffee. Community. A holistic hub for awareness, training, and sustainability.

The Alabaster Jar proposes the establishment of a multi-functional community storefront in the U.S. that serves as a secular, public-facing hub to support anti-trafficking efforts both domestically and internationally.

This space will include a large bookstore, coffee shop, rentable conference rooms, and co-working spaces that foster community engagement and education while generating revenue to support our global field operations.

Our Vision

More Than a Business

To establish a vibrant and welcoming community hub that cultivates awareness, trains advocates, and generates sustainable income to rescue, restore, and empower survivors of human trafficking.

This hybrid model establishes a financially sustainable foundation that advances our mission of promoting mental health education, raising anti-trafficking awareness, and providing workforce training for those on the front lines.

By providing a secular, welcoming space that resonates with a diverse audience, The Alabaster Jar can reach hearts that might never attend a traditional church or missions conference, yet still yearn to make a difference.
