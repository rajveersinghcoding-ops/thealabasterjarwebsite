Digital Frontline

Cyber Intelligence Operations

Using advanced technology and law enforcement partnerships to identify, intercept, and disrupt trafficking operations before victims are exploited.

Human trafficking has increasingly moved into the digital realm, with traffickers utilizing online platforms, social media, encrypted messaging applications, and the dark web to recruit, advertise, and exploit victims. The Alabaster Jar's Cyber Intelligence Unit serves as the frontline defense against these digital threats.

Our cyber intelligence operations focus initially on the United States and Thailand, two critical regions in the global trafficking landscape. The United States serves as both a source and destination country for trafficking victims, while Thailand remains a significant hub for sex trafficking and labor exploitation in Southeast Asia. Our operations are designed to scale to Mexico, Colombia, India, and other regions as capacity develops.

How It Works

Our Operational Framework

The Cyber Intelligence Unit operates through four integrated functional areas.

Digital Surveillance & Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of known trafficking platforms and digital spaces where exploitation occurs.

  • Monitor escort websites and classified advertisement sites for trafficking indicators
  • Social media surveillance using advanced analytics to identify recruitment patterns
  • Dark web monitoring to track trafficking marketplaces and forums
  • Geo-location tracking and pattern analysis for trafficking routes
  • Image recognition technology to identify missing persons and repeat victims

Intelligence Analysis & Investigation Support

Transforming raw data into actionable intelligence for law enforcement partners.

  • Open-source intelligence (OSINT) gathering and analysis
  • Development of actionable intelligence packages
  • Link analysis mapping connections between traffickers, victims, and locations
  • Trend analysis identifying emerging trafficking methods
  • Secure databases of trafficking indicators and case information

Law Enforcement Partnership & Coordination

Building formal relationships with agencies for effective collaboration.

  • Formal MOUs with FBI, DHS, and local police departments (U.S.)
  • Partnerships with Royal Thai Police and Department of Special Investigation
  • Coordination with INTERPOL for cross-border investigations
  • Real-time intelligence sharing protocols
  • Joint training exercises and capacity building
  • Participation in regional anti-trafficking task forces

Victim Identification & Outreach

Connecting identified victims with rescue and support services.

  • Proactive online outreach to potential victims
  • Survivor-led peer outreach teams for engagement
  • Trusted referral pathways to local rescue services
  • 24/7 hotline monitoring and response integration
  • Collaboration with National Human Trafficking Hotline (U.S.) and Thailand's 1300 Hotline

Technology Infrastructure

  • Advanced web scraping and data aggregation tools

Our Team

Staffing & Training

Our cyber intelligence team comprises specialized professionals committed to the mission.

Cyber Intelligence Director

Oversees all digital operations, law enforcement relationships, and strategic direction.

Senior Analysts

Experienced investigators specializing in OSINT, dark web operations, and trafficking pattern analysis.

Digital Investigators

Conduct daily monitoring, surveillance, and initial analysis of potential cases.

Law Enforcement Liaisons

Former law enforcement professionals managing partner agency relationships.

Victim Advocates

Trained professionals specializing in trauma-informed online victim engagement.

Technical Support

Maintain technology infrastructure and develop custom analytical tools.

Training Requirements

All team members undergo rigorous training including:

  • Trauma-informed investigation techniques
  • Legal frameworks in U.S. and Thai jurisdictions
  • Operational security protocols
  • Evidence preservation and chain of custody
  • Cultural competency training
  • Vicarious trauma prevention
Our Standards

Ethical & Legal Compliance

All cyber intelligence operations adhere to strict ethical and legal standards:

  1. 1 Operations conducted within legal frameworks, with legal counsel review
  2. 2 Victim privacy and dignity are paramount with strict confidentiality protocols
  3. 3 Intelligence shared only with verified law enforcement through secure channels
  4. 4 Quarterly reviews by an independent ethics board
  5. 5 Background checks and confidentiality agreements for all staff
  6. 6 Data retention policies with secure destruction when no longer needed
