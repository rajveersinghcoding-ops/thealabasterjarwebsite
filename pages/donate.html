Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! The Alabaster Jar is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly funds our programs: rescue operations, survivor rehabilitation, prevention education, and our cyber intelligence unit. We maintain 85% program efficiency, meaning 85 cents of every dollar goes directly to our mission work.

Can I designate my gift to a specific program?

Yes, you can designate your gift to a specific area such as Cyber Intelligence Operations, Survivor Rehabilitation, Prevention Programs, or the Book House Project. Please note this in the comments when donating or contact us directly.

How do I cancel or modify my monthly donation?

You can modify or cancel your monthly giving at any time by contacting us at donations@thealabasterjar.org or calling our office. We appreciate your support at whatever level works for you.
