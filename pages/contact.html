Common Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. Urgent matters related to trafficking reports should be directed to the National Human Trafficking Hotline (1-888-373-7888) for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a tour or visit your offices?

We welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to schedule a visit. Note that some of our locations are confidential for the safety of survivors.

How can I book a speaker for my church or event?

We have team members available to speak at churches, schools, businesses, and community events. Please email speaking@thealabasterjar.org with details about your event, including date, location, audience size, and topic of interest.
