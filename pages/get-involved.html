Raise Your Voice

Become an Advocate

You don't have to travel overseas to make a difference. Become an advocate for the voiceless right where you are.

Ways to Advocate:

  • Learn: Educate yourself about trafficking signs and statistics
  • Share: Use social media to spread awareness
  • Report: Know the hotlines and how to report suspicious activity
  • Shop Ethically: Support businesses that fight trafficking
  • Host Events: Organize awareness gatherings in your community
  • Contact Officials: Advocate for anti-trafficking legislation

Know the Signs

Common indicators of human trafficking:

  • Signs of physical abuse or neglect
  • Avoiding eye contact, fearful behavior
  • Not in control of their own ID or finances
  • Coached or scripted responses
  • Living with employer or in poor conditions
  • Working excessive hours

National Human Trafficking Hotline:

1-888-373-7888

Text "HELP" to 233733
